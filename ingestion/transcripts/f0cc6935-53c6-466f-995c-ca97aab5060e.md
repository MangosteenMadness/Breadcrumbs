Here is an overview of all available data across the platform:

---

## 🗄️ Multi-Omics Datasets (Plotter)

Four datasets are available, each with multiple data modalities:

| Dataset | Modalities |
|---|---|
| **MOSAIC** | Bulk RNA-seq, scRNA-seq, Spatial transcriptomics, Clinical data, WES/Variants, Histomics metadata, Measurement & procedure events |
| **TCGA** | Bulk RNA-seq, Clinical data, WES/Variants, Histomics metadata, Sample metadata |
| **GTEx** | Bulk RNA-seq, Clinical data, Sample metadata |
| **CPTAC** | Bulk RNA-seq, Proteomics, Clinical data, WES/Variants, Sample metadata |

---

## 🔬 Histopathology (Pathology Explorer)

27 whole-slide image cohorts are available with H&E-based AI features (cell densities, spatial features, survival analysis):

- **IBD**: `IBDColEpi`
- **TCGA** (26 cancer types): `TCGA_ACC`, `TCGA_BLCA`, `TCGA_BRCA`, `TCGA_CESC`, `TCGA_CHOL`, `TCGA_COAD`, `TCGA_DLBC`, `TCGA_ESCA`, `TCGA_HNSC`, `TCGA_KICH`, `TCGA_KIRC`, `TCGA_KIRP`, `TCGA_LIHC`, `TCGA_LUAD`, `TCGA_LUSC`, `TCGA_MESO`, `TCGA_OV`, `TCGA_PAAD`, `TCGA_PRAD`, `TCGA_READ`, `TCGA_SARC`, `TCGA_STAD`, `TCGA_THCA`, `TCGA_THYM`, `TCGA_UCEC`, `TCGA_UCS`

---

## 🧬 Prior Knowledge (BioKnowledge Navigator)

Gene-level biological annotations from public databases covering: Essentiality, Immunology, Localisation, Oncogenicity, Ontology, Protein features, RNA, Safety, Specificity, Tolerability, Tractability, and Transcription Factors.

---

## 📊 Available Analyses

Over 20 analyses are supported, including: differential expression (bulk DESeq2, scRNA pseudobulk, proteomics t-test), survival (Kaplan-Meier, Cox/Forest), trajectory, enrichment (ssGSEA/GSEA), cell-cell communication, clustering, mutation frequency, spatial transcriptomics visualization, and more.
