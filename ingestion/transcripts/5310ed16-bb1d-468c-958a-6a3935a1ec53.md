**Yes — a subset of MOSAIC DLBCL tumors shows spatially organized CXCL13+ structures, with clear inter-patient heterogeneity.**

## Approach
CXCL13 was confirmed present in the MOSAIC spatial transcriptomics (Visium) count table across all 10 DLBCL patients (PAT_D_* IDs). CXCL13 expression per spot was quantified, and Moran's I spatial autocorrelation was used to determine whether high-CXCL13 spots cluster (indicative of organized follicle-like aggregates) vs. scatter randomly.

B cell deconvolution fractions were queried but were uninformative: in DLBCL, the malignant B cells are captured under the 'malignant' deconvolution label and not the 'B_cell' reference fraction — this is expected biologically.

## Key Findings

### Patients with spatially organized CXCL13+ aggregates (Moran's I significant)

**1. PAT_D_XSMK1IY6 — strongest signal**
- Mean CXCL13 = 6.39 log-norm, p50 = 6.81 (median spot expresses CXCL13 highly)
- 95% of spots are CXCL13-positive (only 95/2048 zeros)
- **Moran's I = 0.402, p < 0.001** → significant positive spatial autocorrelation
- The slide shows near-pan-tumor high CXCL13 with superimposed spatial clustering — consistent with dense, organized CXCL13+ B cell follicle-like niche architecture
- Clinical: IPI 4-5, CD10−, achieved complete response to immuno-chemotherapy

**2. PAT_D_PNCXN8EG — focal structured aggregates**
- Mean CXCL13 = 3.68, ~71% spots CXCL13+
- **Moran's I = 0.434, p < 0.001** — the strongest spatial clustering coefficient in the cohort
- ~29% of spots (1241/4312) are CXCL13-negative — creating a clear focal/patchy pattern characteristic of discrete follicle-like structures
- Clinical: ABC subtype, centroblastic, BCL6+, MUM1+, IPI 4-5 — had progressive disease on chemotherapy

### High-expression but spatially diffuse
**3. PAT_D_EBUUXOGN**
- Mean CXCL13 = 3.12, ~57% spots positive
- **Moran's I = 0.076, p = 0.26 (non-significant)** — CXCL13 expression is high but randomly distributed
- Suggests pan-tumor CXCL13 transcription without organized follicular architecture
- Clinical: ABC subtype, BCL6+, MUM1+, IPI 4-5, achieved complete response

**4. PAT_D_78HSE9YV / PAT_D_92HZIDVT / PAT_D_JNVZAT4N**
- Moderate to bimodal CXCL13 distributions (means 1.94–3.77); spatial clustering not formally tested here — recommend running individual Moran's I analyses

### CXCL13-low patients
**PAT_D_2XMFLJKS, PAT_D_XE04CCF0, PAT_D_7K2O8C2E, PAT_D_YVILG78D** all have median CXCL13 = 0 with few positive spots — no evidence of follicle-like CXCL13+ structures.

## Interpretation
CXCL13 in DLBCL marks the germinal center microenvironment and follicular helper T cells (TFH), not solely malignant B cells. Spatially clustered CXCL13+ domains (as in PAT_D_XSMK1IY6 and PAT_D_PNCXN8EG) are consistent with **organized TLS/follicle-like niches** within the tumor microenvironment. The bimodal distributions in PAT_D_92HZIDVT and PAT_D_JNVZAT4N (p50=0 but p75 ~6.1–6.3) suggest focal pockets of CXCL13+ cells embedded in CXCL13-negative tumor areas — also a hallmark of focal TLS-like structures.

Notably, PAT_D_PNCXN8EG had progressive disease on chemotherapy despite its structured CXCL13+ architecture, while PAT_D_XSMK1IY6 achieved complete response — suggesting CXCL13 spatial organization alone may not be sufficient to predict response.

Three spatial transcriptomics analyses were performed on the MOSAIC DLBCL cohort (n=10 patients, all PAT_D_* identifiers):

1. **CXCL13 expression violin per patient** (Figure 1): Wilcoxon pairwise comparisons confirm PAT_D_XSMK1IY6 has significantly higher CXCL13 than all others (all adj. p<0.001). PAT_D_PNCXN8EG and PAT_D_78HSE9YV form a second tier. Lowest expressors are PAT_D_YVILG78D and PAT_D_7K2O8C2E.

2. **Spatial slide map — PAT_D_XSMK1IY6** (Figure 2): n=2048 spots, 95% CXCL13+, mean 6.39. Moran's I=0.402, p<0.001 → significant spatial clustering.

3. **Spatial slide map — PAT_D_PNCXN8EG** (Figure 3): n=4312 spots, ~71% CXCL13+, mean 3.68. Moran's I=0.434, p<0.001 → strongest clustering coefficient.

4. **Spatial slide map — PAT_D_EBUUXOGN** (Figure 4): n=4418 spots, ~57% CXCL13+, mean 3.12. Moran's I=0.076, p=0.26 → no significant spatial clustering despite high mean expression.

B cell deconvolution fractions were uninformative for DLBCL: malignant B cells are captured in the 'malignant' deconvolution compartment, not the 'B_cell' reference.
