## CXCL13⁺ B-cell Spatial Organization in MOSAIC DLBCL Tumors

**Overview (10 DLBCL patients with spatial transcriptomics):**
All 10 patients express both CXCL13 and MS4A1 (CD20) at the transcript level. However, **spatial colocalization** (LIANA bivariate cosine similarity between CXCL13 and MS4A1 neighborhood expression) — the critical criterion for follicle-like organized structures — is highly variable and present in only **3 patients**:

---

### Patients WITH follicle-like CXCL13⁺ B-cell organization:
| Patient | Mean CXCL13 (log-norm) | Mean MS4A1 | Spatial colocalization score | Fraction of spots significant | COO subtype |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| **PAT_D_XSMK1IY6** | 6.70 | 6.24 | **0.53** (strongest) | **41%** | — |
| **PAT_D_PNCXN8EG** | 5.16 | 6.99 | **0.30** | **26%** | GCB |
| **PAT_D_78HSE9YV** | 5.54 | 7.76 | **0.15** | **11%** | — |

### Patients WITHOUT significant spatial colocalization (dispersed expression only):
| Patient | CXCL13 mean | MS4A1 mean | Colocalization |
|---|---|---|---|
| PAT_D_YVILG78D | 6.90 (highest!) | 8.29 (highest!) | 0.0 — fully dispersed |
| PAT_D_EBUUXOGN | 5.50 | 6.47 | 0.0 |
| PAT_D_JNVZAT4N | 6.64 | 6.83 | 0.0 |
| PAT_D_92HZIDVT | 5.99 | 7.07 | 0.0 |
| PAT_D_XE04CCF0 | 5.20 | 5.39 | 0.0 |
| PAT_D_2XMFLJKS | 4.82 | 6.83 | 0.05 (minimal) |
| PAT_D_7K2O8C2E | 6.75 | 7.50 | 0.05 (minimal) |

---

**Key insight:** High CXCL13 or MS4A1 expression alone does NOT predict spatial colocalization.
