The SYPL1 spatial transcriptomics dotplot (MOSAIC, n=77 patients across 8 indications) reveals clear cell-type and indication-specific expression patterns:

**Highest expression in Malignant cells:**
- **BLCA (bladder):** Malignant spots show the highest SYPL1 expression overall across the dataset (mean log-norm ≈ 3.17, ~68% expressing), significantly higher than all other cell types (adj. p < 0.0001 vs. Epithelial_other, MoMac, Muscle, T_NK, etc.)
- **HNSC (head & neck):** Malignant cells are the top expressors (mean ≈ 3.76, ~76%), significantly above Fibroblast, Granulocyte, Muscle, and Stromal_other (adj. p < 0.0001)
- **OV (ovarian):** Malignant spots have high expression (mean ≈ 3.04, ~73%), with Epithelial_other and Epithelial_secretory spots also notably high (~78% and ~75%, mean ≈ 3.44 and 3.12)
- **NSCLC:** Malignant cells are highest (mean ≈ 2.01, ~49%), significantly above Fibroblast, Endothelial, T_NK, and Plasma
- **MESO:** Malignant spots express SYPL1 (mean ≈ 2.19, ~53%), significantly above Fibroblast, MoMac, and Stromal_other
- **BRCA:** Malignant cells lead (mean ≈ 2.13, ~46%), significantly higher than MoMac, Stromal_other, Granulocyte, and Fibroblast

**Notable non-malignant expression:**
- **HNSC Fibroblasts:** Unusually elevated SYPL1 (mean ≈ 2.74, ~56%), the highest fibroblast expression across all indications — significantly above Stromal_other (adj. p < 0.0001)
- **DLBCL T_NK cells:** High expression (mean ≈ 2.30, ~56%), ranking as the top-expressing cell type in this lymphoma indication, significantly above Malignant, MoMac, Fibroblast, and Stromal_other
- **HNSC Plasma & MoMac cells:** Notably elevated (mean ≈ 2.68 and 3.12 respectively), both significantly above Stromal_other and Muscle
- **GBM:** Expression is spread across brain-specific cell types — Neurons (100% expressing, mean ≈ 3.84, very low n=3), Oligodendrocytes (~43%, mean ≈ 1.81), Excitatory_Glutamatergic (~45%, mean ≈ 1.75), Microglia (~42%, mean ≈ 1.77), and Malignant (~43%, mean ≈ 1.78)

**Low expressors across all indications:**
- Granulocytes, Mast cells, Stromal_other, and B cells consistently show the lowest SYPL1 expression and percent expressing across most indications

In summary, **SYPL1 is predominantly expressed in malignant cells** across most solid tumors in MOSAIC, with particularly high levels in BLCA, HNSC, and OV. Noteworthy exception: in **DLBCL**, T_NK cells surpass malignant cells in SYPL1 expression; and in **HNSC**, fibroblasts and immune cells (MoMac, Plasma) are also highly enriched.

The sparse_aggregation_analysis dotplot (MOSAIC spatial transcriptomics, baseline primary tumor/metastatic samples) displays SYPL1 mean log-normalized expression (dot color) and percent of spots expressing (dot size) across 21 cell types (y-axis) and 8 indications (x-axis). Key quantitative highlights:

- **BLCA Malignant:** mean = 3.17, 67.6% expressing (n = 23,901 spots) — highest malignant expression in dataset
- **HNSC Malignant:** mean = 3.76, 76.0% (n = 7,335) — highest across all cell type × indication combinations
- **OV Epithelial_other:** mean = 3.44, 77.9% (n = 86)
- **OV Malignant:** mean = 3.04, 73.0% (n = 17,416)
- **HNSC MoMac:** mean = 3.12, 62.9% (n = 2,729)
- **DLBCL T_NK:** mean = 2.30, 55.8% (n = 1,452) — top cell type in DLBCL
- **GBM Neuron:** mean = 3.84, 100% (n = 3; low confidence due to small n)
- **Granulocytes / Mast / Stromal_other:** consistently < 0.2 mean expression and < 20% expressing across all indications

Pairwise Wilcoxon tests (FDR-corrected) confirm 485 significant contrasts. The Malignant–Stromal_other contrast in HNSC shows the largest mean difference (+3.24), followed by Malignant–Granulocyte in HNSC (+2.85).

## K Pro

The analysis was performed on the **MOSAIC** dataset (Multi-Omics Spatial Atlas In Cancer) — specifically its spatial transcriptomics (Visium) modality, using the `mosaic__sptrnaseq_count_table` and `mosaic__sptrnaseq_metadata_table` tables.

MOSAIC is a curated, publicly accessible multimodal dataset from **Owkin**, covering **100 cancer patients across 8 tumor types**: BLCA, OV, GBM, DLBCL, MESO, NSCLC, HNSCC, and BRCA. It integrates spatial transcriptomics, single-cell and bulk RNA-seq, whole-exome sequencing, H&E histology, and curated clinical data. The analysis was restricted to baseline primary tumor and metastatic site samples.
